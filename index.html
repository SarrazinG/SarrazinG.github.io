I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at McGill University. I work in Jérôme Waldispühl's group for Computational Structural Biology.

My research focuses on algorithmic and statistical aproaches to modelling and predicting RNA 3D structure, with a focus on the prediction of specific substructures named RNA 3D modules. I am also interested in human computing and citizen science: my main project as of Winter 2020 focuses on developing a crowd-computing game for large scale analysis of RNA sequence data.
